Who we are

Chica Mob Quarterly is a literary manga project created and maintained by six friends that live in New York City: cass, david, kathy, lee, maisy, and quasimatt.

FAQ

Answers to common questions

What is Changa?

Changa is a form of manga. When a group of six friends, called chicas, come together and use the power of their friendship to draw manga, “Changa” occurs. This is because it is a creative mixture of the words “Chica” and “Manga.”
